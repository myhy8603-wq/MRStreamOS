# Amazon Integration Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to integrate Amazon services within MRStreamOS, including account linking, device activation, and safety requirements.

---

## 1. Account Creation
Users must create Amazon accounts ONLY through the official Amazon website:
https://www.amazon.com

MRStreamOS does not provide account creation, activation, or paid services.

---

## 2. Device Activation
To activate Amazon Prime Video or Fire TV:
1. Open the official activation page:
   https://www.amazon.com/code
2. Enter the code shown on your device.
3. Sign in with your Amazon account.

---

## 3. Safety Notes
- Never pay any third party for Amazon activation.
- MRStreamOS does not sell, rent, or activate Amazon accounts.
- Always use official Amazon links.

---

## 4. Support
For issues, visit:
https://www.amazon.com/gp/help
