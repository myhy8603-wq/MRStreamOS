# Netflix Integration Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to integrate Netflix services within MRStreamOS, including device activation, account safety, and official access requirements.

---

## 1. Account Creation
Users must create Netflix accounts ONLY through the official Netflix website:
https://www.netflix.com

MRStreamOS does not create, activate, sell, or rent Netflix accounts.

---

## 2. Device Activation
To activate Netflix on any device:
1. Open the Netflix app on your device.
2. Sign in using your official Netflix account.
3. If a code appears, enter it at:
   https://www.netflix.com/activate

---

## 3. Safety Notes
- Never pay any third party for Netflix activation.
- MRStreamOS does not provide login credentials or activation services.
- Always use official Netflix links and apps.

---

## 4. Support
For issues, visit:
https://help.netflix.com
