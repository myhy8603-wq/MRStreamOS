# Affiliate Model — MRStreamOS

## Overview
The MRStreamOS Affiliate Model provides a safe, legal, and scalable way to generate revenue without handling user payments directly. All earnings come from official partner programs such as Amazon, Microsoft, Netflix, GitHub, and others.

---

## Why This Model
- Zero legal risk  
- No handling of user money  
- No subscriptions or billing systems  
- Revenue comes from partner companies  
- Fully compliant with global standards  
- Perfect for a platform built on integrations and guides  

---

## How It Works
1. Users access MRStreamOS to find official guides and safe entry points.  
2. They click on verified partner links.  
3. If they subscribe, purchase, or activate a service, the partner company pays MRStreamOS a commission.  
4. Users never pay MRStreamOS directly.  

---

## Benefits
- Safe and transparent  
- Scalable with traffic  
- No customer support burden  
- No financial infrastructure required  
- Works globally  

---

## Notes
MRStreamOS does not sell accounts, does not charge users, and does not store payment information. All financial interactions occur directly with partner companies.
