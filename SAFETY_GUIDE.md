# Global Safety & Anti‑Fraud Guide

## Overview
This guide defines the global safety rules of MRStreamOS, including user protection, anti‑fraud policies, and platform‑wide compliance standards.

---

## 1. Official Access Only
Users must access all platforms ONLY through their official websites:
- Amazon: https://www.amazon.com
- Netflix: https://www.netflix.com
- Microsoft: https://www.microsoft.com
- GitHub: https://github.com

MRStreamOS does not provide accounts, activation, or paid services for any platform.

---

## 2. Anti‑Fraud Rules
To protect users:
- Never pay any third party for activation or login.
- Never share passwords, codes, or tokens.
- Avoid unofficial apps, websites, or activation pages.
- MRStreamOS does not request personal data or payments.

---

## 3. Device Safety
- Always activate devices using official links.
- Avoid unknown QR codes or suspicious activation pages.
- MRStreamOS does not unlock, crack, or modify devices.

---

## 4. User Protection
MRStreamOS enforces:
- Zero tolerance for scams.
- Zero tolerance for account selling or renting.
- Zero tolerance for unauthorized access.

---

## 5. Support
For safety concerns:
- Contact the official support of the platform you are using.
